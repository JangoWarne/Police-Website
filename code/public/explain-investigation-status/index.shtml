My Bikes / Investigation Status



Status: Open


This status shows when the bicycle is reported as stolen but no police officer has been allocated to the case.

It will typically show for the first few days after reporting a bicycle as stolen.


Status: Closed


This status shows when the investigation for this bicycle has concluded.

It may show temporarily between the bicycle being found and being marked as no longer stolen.


Status: Under Investigation


This status shows when a police officer has been allocated to finding your bicycle.

It will show for as long as your bicycle in under active investigation.


Status: Under Investigation - Contacting User


This status shows when a police officer needs to contact you.

It will show until the officer is successful in contacting you.
