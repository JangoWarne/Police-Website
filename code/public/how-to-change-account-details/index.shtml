How to change your details

  1. Move the mouse cursor to "Account" in the top right corner so that the dropdown appears.


  2. Click on "My Account Details" in the list.


  3. Change or add to your personal details.

    • If you do not want to change your password leave "Password" and "Confirm Password" empty.


      • The following information is required and must be filled in:

    • Title
    • First Name
    • Last Name
    • Email
    • Address Line 1
    • Town/City
    • Postcode


  4. Click "Save Changes" at the bottom of the page.

    • If you changed your password you will be asked to enter your password to confirm the changes.
